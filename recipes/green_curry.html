Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Coconut Milk
  • green beans

  • green curry paste

  • red chili

Steps

  1. cut all ingredients in tiny slices or cubes
  2. put onions, garlic, chili and curry paste in a pot and stir
  3. add coconut milk, water and let it boil
  4. add the other ingredients and cook until everything is ready to eat
  5. Enjoy!
