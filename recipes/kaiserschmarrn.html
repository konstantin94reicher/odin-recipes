Ingredients

  • 250g Milk
  • 4 Eggs
  • 200g Flour
  • 1TS Sugar

Steps

  1. Separate the eggs and mix the egg whites to a solid mass
  2. Mix egg yolks, milk flour and sugar
  3. Add the egg white mass
  4. pour everything all together in a pan an let it cook until its a solid mass. Separate the mass into tiny pieces
  5. Enjoy!
