Ingredients

  • Pork escalopes
  • flour
  • eggs
  • bread crumbs
  • plant-based oil

Steps

  1. break open the eggs into a deep plate and stir until they are mixed
  2. put flour and bread crumbs into two extra plates
  3. batter the escalopes until they're really flat
  4. flip the escalopes in flour, then the eggs, then the bread crumbs
  5. put the oil in a pan and turn it really hot
  6. put the escalopes in the oil and let them fry until they're really crunchy
  7. Enjoy!
